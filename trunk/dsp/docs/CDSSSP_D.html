Introduction

This component is an implementation of the increase-only single-source shortest paths algorithm (up to distance D) described in: V. King,"Fully dynamic algorithms for maintaining all-pairs shortest paths and transitive closure in digraphs", in Proceedings of the 40-th Annual IEEE Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science (FOCS'99), New York City, NY, pp. 81-99, 1999. The algorithm is implemented following some of the space reduction techniques described in: V.King and M.Thorup, "A space saving trick for directed dynamic transitive closure and shortest path algorithms", in Proceedings of the 7th Annual International Computing and Combinatorics Conference (COCOON'01), Guilin, China, LNCS 2108, pp. 268-277, 2001.
